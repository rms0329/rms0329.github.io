Donggeun Lim

I am a Ph.D. student at 3D Vision Lab in Seoul National University, under the supervision of Prof. Young Min Kim.

My research interest lies in computer graphics and animation, especially in 3D human motion generation. Recently, I am particularly interested in developing methods for synthesizing realistic human interaction motions in diverse environments.

Before joining to 3D Vision Lab, I worked at Samsung Electronics and earned my Bachelor's degree from Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Seoul National University.
