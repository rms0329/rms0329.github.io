Hyeongjun Heo

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Seoul National University under the supervision of Prof. Young Min Kim.

My research interests span computer vision, computer graphics, and robotics. I am broadly interested in developing systems that can perceive and understand the 3D world, with the goal of enabling intelligent interaction with complex physical environments. Recently, I am particularly focused on methods that enhance spatial reasoning and generalization in real-world scenarios.
